Rice and Beans Recipe

This type of food is really popular and very delicious. it is easy to prepare and healthy to eat.

Ingredients

Here are the step by step on how to cook it for your family or guests


  • 1 cup of rice
  • 1 cup of beans (cooked)
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin (optional)
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. In a pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent.
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  4. Add the rinsed rice to the pot and stir well to coat with the oil.
  5. Pour in 2 cups of water (or vegetable broth) and bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 15-20 minutes or until the rice is cooked and water is absorbed.
  7. In a separate pot, heat the cooked beans with cumin, salt, and pepper.
  8. Once the rice is done, fluff it with a fork and mix in the beans.
  9. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Serve the rice and beans warm as a main dish or side dish. Enjoy your meal!

Notes

This recipe can be customized by adding vegetables like bell peppers or carrots, or by using different spices according to your taste.

Tips

For a richer flavor, you can cook the rice in vegetable or chicken broth instead of water. You can also add a squeeze of lemon juice before serving for an extra zing.

Storage

Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat in the microwave or on the stove with a splash of water to prevent drying out.

Variations

Feel free to experiment with different types of beans, such as black beans or kidney beans, and add spices like paprika or chili powder for a kick. You can also serve this dish with a side of yogurt or a fresh salad.

Serving Suggestions

This rice and beans dish pairs well with grilled meats, roasted vegetables, or a simple green salad. It can also be served as a filling for wraps or burritos.

Nutrition Information

This dish is a good source of protein, fiber, and essential nutrients. The combination of rice and beans provides a complete protein, making it a nutritious option for vegetarians and vegans.

  • Frequently Asked Questions

      Yes, you can use canned beans. Just rinse and drain them before adding to the recipe.

      Yes, this recipe is already vegan-friendly as it does not contain any animal products.
    3. How can I make this dish spicier?
      You can add chopped jalapeños or a pinch of cayenne pepper to increase the heat level.
